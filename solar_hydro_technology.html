Solar Hydro Distiller

PI: Prof. Anand B. Rao / Prof. Vishal Sardeshpande,C-TARA
Inventor: Mr. Vikrant Katekar, C-TARA, IIT Bombay

The intervention aims to provide a sustainable, fuel-free distillation solution for producing hydrosols and extracts, fostering economic opportunities while promoting environmental sustainability.

Empowering Rural Communities Through Natural Extracts Production

Unlocking the Potential of Natural Extracts in Rural Areas

Rural communities face challenges in accessing sustainable and affordable methods to produce natural extracts and hydrosols. These limitations prevent them from utilizing available natural resources effectively, impacting their ability to diversify their income sources.

Missed Opportunities in the Organic Market

The lack of infrastructure and technology restricts rural producers from entering the growing market for eco-friendly and organic products. This missed opportunity hinders their economic growth and ability to contribute to sustainable practices.

Salient features: What Sets It Apart
  • Operates without fuel, relying solely on solar energy
  • Easy to use with minimal monitoring requirements
  • Produces a variety of hydrosols and extracts
  • Provides a complementary income source alongside agriculture

RuTAG IITB Intervention

Versatile Solar Distillation Unit

The solar hydro distiller is a solar thermal distillation system designed to produce a variety of natural extracts such as rose water, ginger, neem, tulsi, and mint. Its versatile application supports the growing demand for natural and eco-friendly products.

Cost-Effective and User-Friendly Technology

This innovative unit is simple to use and requires no constant monitoring or external fuel, making it highly efficient and affordable. It provides a sustainable livelihood option for rural populations, reducing dependency on costly energy sources.

Adoption & Impact
  • The solar hydro distiller is operational in 18 units across Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh, and Chhattisgarh
  • Reduces dependence on fossil fuels, promoting environmental sustainability
  • Helps rural communities produce high-demand natural products, boosting income
  • Taps into the growing hydrosols market, enhancing long-term viability
