Solar Charge Controller

PI: Prof. Anand B. Rao,C-TARA
Inventor: Mr. Rajarshi Sen, Customized energy solutions, Prof. Satish Agnihotri, C-TARA

The intervention aims to enhance the efficiency and lifespan of lead-acid batteries in rural solar mini-grids using intelligent charge controllers, ensuring reliable energy access, reducing operational costs, and promoting sustainable development in rural communities

Optimizing Solar Mini-Grids for Rural Development

Challenges with Solar Mini-Grids in Rural India

In rural India, solar-based mini-grids with lead-acid batteries face problems like inefficient charging and discharging. These issues reduce battery life, require frequent replacements, and lead to unreliable power supply. Poor battery monitoring also increases operational costs and creates more electronic waste.

Need for an Efficient Battery Management Solution

There is a need for a smart, low-cost solution to improve battery performance and lifespan. This will ensure reliable energy for rural communities, reduce costs, and support economic growth while improving overall living conditions.

Salient features: What Sets It Apart
  • This technology is easy to install at the existing as well as new solar based mini-grids with lead acid battery banks
  • Improves battery life and battery charging efficiency by average 20% for heavy load grids
  • One intelligent charge controller can cater to battery bank of one mini-grid

RuTAG IITB Intervention

Role of RuTAG in Technology Deployment

RuTAG can help introduce intelligent charge controllers for lead-acid battery banks in rural India. By supporting technology transfer and conducting pilot projects, RuTAG can address critical challenges in solar mini-grids.

Collaborative Efforts for Better Energy Solutions

By working with local communities, government bodies, and energy stakeholders, RuTAG can show how this technology improves battery performance and energy reliability.

Adoption & Impact
  • Enhanced Energy Access: Improves energy reliability in solar-based mini-grids for rural communities
  • Battery Performance: Increases battery life and charging efficiency by 20%, reducing operational costs
  • Affordability and Sustainability: Extends battery lifespan, making solar energy systems more cost-effective and eco-friendly
  • Easy Integration: Compatible with both existing and new solar mini-grids, ensuring seamless adoption
  • Support for Quick Adoption: Backed by government initiatives and NGO partnerships for widespread implementation
  • Impact on Rural Development: Provides reliable electricity for households, schools, and healthcare centers
  • Economic Growth: Supports small businesses and fosters economic opportunities in rural areas
  • Environmental Benefits: Reduces electronic waste and promotes sustainable energy practices
  • Improved Quality of Life: Ensures better living conditions and access to essential services
