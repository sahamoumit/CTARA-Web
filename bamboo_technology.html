Bamboo Polyhouse

PI: Prof. Chharuchandra Korde,C-TARA
Inventor: Ms. Namrata Bhansali, C-TARA, IIT Bombay

The objective is to provide a cost-effective, durable, and sustainable alternative to traditional playhouses. This intervention aims to empower farmers to grow high-value crops while reducing initial investment and maintenance costs

Affordable Alternatives to Traditional Playhouses

Challenges with Metal Pipe Playhouses

Traditional playhouses made from metal pipes are costly, making them out of reach for small and marginal farmers. The high expense discourages widespread use among those who need them most for farming and related activities

Weather Vulnerability and Limited Utility

Metal pipe playhouses often cannot endure harsh weather conditions, reducing their durability and usefulness. This limitation increases risks for farmers, as these structures may fail when needed the most.

Salient features: What Sets It Apart
  • Strength and stability-tested technology
  • Withstands heavy wind speeds and rains
  • Cost-effective design using locally available materials

RuTAG IITB Intervention

Low-Cost Bamboo Playhouse for Farmers

The Bamboo Playhouse is an affordable polyhouse made using locally available bamboo. This cost-effective structure provides a viable alternative to expensive metal playhouses, making it accessible for small and marginal farmers.

Durable and Sustainable Farming Solution

The playhouse has been tested for strength and stability to withstand extreme weather, ensuring reliability throughout the year. Farmers can grow vegetables, bell peppers, and exotic herbs, boosting their income while supporting sustainable agricultural practices.

Adoption & Impact
  • Ten Bamboo Playhouses have been distributed in Maharashtra, benefiting small-scale farmers
  • Farmers use these playhouses to grow high-demand crops, increasing profitability and income stability
  • The structures are affordable and durable, making them suitable for rural areas
  • This intervention promotes sustainable farming and improves farmer livelihoods
  • Bamboo Playhouses provide a cost-effective alternative to expensive metal polyhouses
