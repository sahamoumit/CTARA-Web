Gassifier Based Community Cooking System

The goal is to convert surplus agro-residue into fuel in form of pellets using a pelletizing machine. The pellets are used in patented gasifier technology for generation of heat energy for cooking. (Mainly for community cooking needs e.g. Ashram Shalas). This reduces air pollution, replacing a significant portion of crude oil imports, and fostering sustainable energy solutions

Agro-Residue: An Untapped Resource

Agro-Residue Production in India

India produces more than 300 million metric tons of agro-residue every year. This massive byproduct of agriculture remains largely underutilized, leaving its potential untapped.

Environmental and Economic Challenges

A significant portion of agro-residue is burned, causing serious air pollution and harming the environment. Its underuse as a fuel source also leads to missed economic opportunities and inefficiencies.

Salient features: What Sets It Apart
  • Quick start and easy maintenance
  • Handles high ash content up to 20%
  • Char and ash removal system
  • Multi-fuel capability
  • Saves INR 2 lakh per annum in LPG costs
  • Initial price: INR 2.5 lakh with a payback period of less than two years

RuTAG IITB Intervention

Pelletizing Machine: Turning Agro-Residue into Fuel

The pelletizing machine is designed to convert biomass into fuel pellets suitable for gasifiers. With a production capacity of 25-30 kg per hour, it is compact, portable, and requires only 5 HP of power. It can utilize different types of agro-residues, making it highly versatile. Self-help groups can use this machine to produce affordable fuel pellets, creating economic opportunities in rural areas

Gasifier: A Clean Cooking Solution

The gasifier, developed by the Tata Centre for Technology and Design (TCTD) at IIT Bombay, is designed for community-based cooking. It works seamlessly with the pelletizing machine and provides a clean, efficient, and affordable alternative to conventional fuels. This system is ideal for use in rural areas, offering an eco-friendly solution for large-scale cooking needs

An Environment-Friendly Technology Pair

Together, the pelletizing machine and gasifier create a sustainable energy solution. The combination reduces dependence on traditional fuels, mitigates air pollution, and supports local communities. This innovative approach promotes environmental conservation while enhancing energy access in underserved regions

Adoption & Impact
  • The gasifier can be scaled for use in community kitchens, such as those in Ashram Schools and self-help groups
  • It replaces traditional fuels with agro-residue pellets, reducing air pollution
  • The technology decreases dependency on fossil fuels
  • It promotes sustainable energy use in rural areas
