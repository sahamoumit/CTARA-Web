Marketmirchi –A Digital Marketing Platform

PI: Prof. Satish Agnihotri, C-TARA
Inventor: Smt. Pragati Gokhale, Advisor Rajiv Gandhi Science & Technology Commission

The primary objective is to empower rural producers, including farmers and vendors, by providing a free and open digital platform that enables direct market access, reduces dependence on intermediaries, and enhances their market reach.

Bridging Market Gaps for Rural Producers

Challenges for Rural Producers

Rural producers in India face major obstacles when trying to access online markets. They often depend on middlemen to sell their products, which reduces their profit margins and limits their earnings.

Impact on Growth and Competitiveness

The lack of direct market access prevents rural producers from scaling their businesses and competing effectively in the digital economy. This creates barriers to their growth and reduces opportunities for financial independence.

Salient features: What Sets It Apart
  • Free entry and exit for users
  • Multilingual video tutorials for ease of use
  • In-person training sessions across Madhya Pradesh, Jharkhand, Odisha, and Chhattisgarh
  • Open platform for seamless digital market access

RuTAG IITB Intervention

Empowering Rural Producers with Marketmirchi

Marketmirchi is a free online portal and app designed to support rural producers. It offers multilingual video tutorials and in-person training programs, ensuring accessibility for users from diverse backgrounds. The platform provides an open entry-exit system, allowing producers to join and participate flexibly.

Recognized Agri-Intervention

Acknowledged by Niti Aayog as one of the best agricultural initiatives, Marketmirchi combines digital tools with physical training methods to help rural producers develop the necessary skills for effective online marketing. This innovative approach bridges the gap between rural producers and digital markets.

Adoption & Impact
  • Recognized by Niti Aayog as a top agricultural initiative
  • Combines digital tools and hands-on training for rural producers
  • Teaches essential skills for effective online marketing
  • Bridges the gap between rural producers and digital markets
  • Promotes sustainable economic growth through market access
